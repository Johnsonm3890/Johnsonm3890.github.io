About Me

Hi, I'm a web designer who sees the internet a bit like a garden-full of potential, color, and endless room to grow.
My journey into web design began much like planting my first seed: with curiosity and a desire to build something from the ground up.
Learning HTML and CSS gave me the tools to shape digital spaces, while Python introduced me to the logic and structure that brings those spaces to life.

Just like in gardening, I've learned that patience, experimentation, and attention to detail are key.
Whether I'm tending to a tricky bug in my code or coaxing a reluctant plant to bloom, I find joy in the process of creating and nurturing.
My passion lies in blending natural inspiration with modern design-crafting websites that feel as organic and inviting as a well-loved garden.

Gardening, for me, is a quiet form of creativity.
There's something deeply satisfying about watching a seed become a blossom, or seeing a once-struggling plant thrive with the right care.
It teaches mindfulness, resilience, and the importance of small, consistent efforts-lessons I carry with me into every design project.
It's where I disconnect, recharge, and often find the ideas that later blossom into digital experiences.
